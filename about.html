About Provida

Our Story, Our Values, Our Community

Our Story

Today, Provida stands as a beacon of excellence, hosting international tournaments, developing young talent, and fostering deep friendships across cultures. Our courts have hosted players from France, Vietnam, Thailand, and beyond.

Our Mission

To build an inclusive, world-class pétanque community in Cambodia that celebrates excellence, respects tradition, and creates lasting friendships through the beautiful sport of pétanque.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as the premier pétanque club in Southeast Asia, known for developing champions, promoting sports excellence, and embodying the true spirit of community and sportsmanship.

Meet Our Founder

Neak Oknha Ing Bunhoaw

Founder & President

With an unwavering passion for pétanque since 2005, Khoun Cheng envisioned a place where players of all levels could come together to celebrate this beautiful sport. What began as a dream to unite Cambodia's pétanque community has blossomed into Provida—a thriving hub of excellence, friendship, and international recognition.

His leadership philosophy centers on inclusivity, continuous improvement, and building a legacy that inspires future generations. Today, Provida stands as a testament to his vision—a club where every member, regardless of background or skill level, feels valued and empowered to pursue their passion for pétanque.

Khoun believes that pétanque is more than a sport—it's a bridge between cultures, a celebration of precision and strategy, and a platform for friendship that transcends borders. Under his stewardship, Provida has grown from 50 founding members to over 450 active players, hosting international tournaments and establishing itself as Southeast Asia's premier pétanque destination.
